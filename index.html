Frequently Asked Questions

How is the compliance drain calculated?

We look at standard manual reporting workloads (like IPEDS federal cycles). In our research across universities, teams operating at Level 1 spend between 300 to 400 hours compiling and clean-up data fields manually.

Where does the cloud waste percentage come from?

Higher Education organizations that expand cloud operations without automated scheduled shutdowns or department cost tracking leak on average 15% to 30% of their monthly cloud budgets on idle environments and orphaned storage resources.
